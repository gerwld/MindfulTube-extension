Having troubles with MindfulTube?

Here are some troubleshooting steps you can try:

  • Refresh the YouTube page.
  • Close all tabs and restart your browser.
  • Disable and then re-enable MindfulTube.
  • Disable any other extensions.
  • Uninstall and reinstall MindfulTube.
  • Clear browser cache.

If the problem persists, please email me at pjaworski.dev@gmail.com.

To help me assist you more efficiently, please include the following information:

  • The version of MindfulTube you are using (1.0.0).
  • A screenshot or video demonstrating the problem.
  • A detailed description of the issue.
  • Your browser and its version (e.g., Firefox 86.0).
